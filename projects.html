Projects

This is a showcase of some of the projects I have worked on during my time at Cal Poly. Some projects were completed for school and others for my employers, American Robot Company and The San Luis Obispo Children's Museum.

Further down, I have included some extracurriculars and my internship experience that were fundamental in shaping my goals and work ethic.


RFID Microscope

JavaFX/Arduino/RFID exhibit for kids focused on biological facts and microscopic images.

ZULights Bike Light System

Microcontroller powered, LED system for safer night-time bicycling.

Inventory Application

JavaFX/MySQL inventory application for models, products, and product licenses.

Ambot Website

Informational website for American Robot Company built using HTML, Twitter Bootstrap, and JavaScript.

Animated Stuffed Animal

Arduino powered stuffed giraffe that can move it's head and neck.

Cal Poly Rose Float

Annual Tournament of Roses Parade float design and construction.

Color Lab

Arduino/RFID/LED exhibit focused on teaching children about mixing light colors.

Hearing Challenge

Arduino hearing test exhibit.

Transamerica Internship

Internship in Los Angeles as an internal services software developer.

FIRST Robotics - Team 207

High school robotics club and international competition.
