About Me

From Hawthorne, CA

I was born in Los Angeles, but moved to Hawthorne when I was 5 years old. I attended Hawthorne High School where I took part in several school activities and varsity soccer. I participated in the robotics team (Metalcrafters Team 207) which played a large role in helping me choose computer engineering as a major. I am a first-generation college student and daughter of two proud parents that grew up in El Salvador.

Cal Poly

I recently graduated from Cal Poly with a degree in computer engineering. I visited San Luis Obispo for the first time when I participated in Cal Poly's Polycultural Weekend during my senior year of high school. I had a great time during the 3-day event and I found that I related to Cal Poly's "Learn by Doing" motto. I was able to gain a significant amount of practical experience from the lab components that are part of almost every class.

During my time at Poly I have been involved in WISH (Women in Software and Hardware), Cal Poly Rose Float, SHPE (Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers), and SWE (Society of Women Engineers).

Rock | Alternative | Indie

I am a huge music fan. It is strange to find me not listening to music wherever I am. I like to listen to a variety of genres, but I always find my way back to alt/indie/rock bands such as: Arcade Fire, Muse, Linkin Park, No Doubt, Red Hot Chili Peppers, HAIM, Broods, etc...

TV Shows

On my down time I like to catch up on some of my favorite TV shows. I greatly enjoy watching sci-fi, comedy, and crime/mystery shows. A few favorites: Dexter, The Office, Doctor Who, Silicon Valley, Broadchurch, The Good Wife, Breaking Bad, Game of Thrones, Parks and Recreation, Luther, and CSI: Crime Scene Investigation.

Soccer

I come from a family of soccer players and I continue the family tradition. I have been playing soccer ever since I was 5 years old. Throughout middle school and high school I played as a goalkeeper and sometimes participated in intramural soccer at Cal Poy. Some of my current favorite players include: Landon Donovan, Keylor Navas, Tim Howard, James Rodriguez, Lionel Messi, Cristiano Ronaldo, Robin van Persie, and Gyasi Zardes.
