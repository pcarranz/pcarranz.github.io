Animated Stuffed Animal


This project marked the first time I used a microcontroller. Everyone was tasked with choosing a stuffed animal and getting it to be animated in some way.

I chose to animate a stuffed giraffe and managed to program an Arduino UNO to control two servos that allowed the giraffe to move it's head up-and-down and side-to-side. I wired the servos to two switches so that either movement could be controlled separately.

This was a pretty simple project, but one I had a lot of fun with and it was great being able to showcase the giraffe during Cal Poly's Open House. It was a joy to watch some of the children stop by and interact with Geoffrey. After the open house he was adopted by the Computer Engineering program office where he lies on top of a cabinet which allows me to see him every time I stop by the office.
